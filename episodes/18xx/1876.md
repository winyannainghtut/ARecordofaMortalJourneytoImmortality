# A Record of a Mortal’s Journey to Immortality - Chapter 1876

All of the beetles let out pained cries when struck by those incantation seals and immediately dispersed. Right away, the three Han Lis continued chanting and forming varied hand seals in succession.

The golden beetles seemed to have slipped under some form of restriction. Though they were reluctant, they were forced to fly toward the white threads that had appeared before them and start devouring them.

After a moment they had devoured the countless threads erupting from the giant cauldron at an astonishing pace.

Among the three Han Lis, the one with green skin suddenly opened his mouth, sending out countless specks of silver light.

The specks were only the size of peas, but after falling into the giant cauldron, they quickly transformed into several dozen silver flowers the size of a palm. Their petals shone with silver light, each marked with crimson patterns.

At sight of the flowers, the thirteen beetles let out ecstatic cries and immediately flew to devour them.

Yet whenever one flower vanished, another appeared in its place.

After a while, the beetles had consumed thousands of these silver flowers and their bodies had expanded slightly.

At that moment the golden Han Li suddenly opened his eyes; black flames erupted from his crimson pupils. After several flashes, they transformed into a net of black flames that swept up all the beetles in the cauldron.

A curious sight followed.

All the spirit insects immediately began devouring those black flames too, and it did not take them long for all flames to disappear.

The final Han Li then let out a loud cry and swung both sleeves through the air. A pair of heavy thundering booms and two thick arcs of golden lightning shot out.

The arcs then transformed into two golden lightning nets that enveloped the thirteen beetles.

The booming continued while those nets turned into countless thin arcs, inundating everything with lightning.

The lightning struck the beetles and made the purple patterns on their bodies flicker chaotically, but up close one could see them stretching their limbs to the limit. They seemed to invite as much lightning as possible; despite that they remained unscathed and appeared to enjoy the process.

After a long time, the golden lightning slowly faded away. The Han Li with the halo then opened his eyes. The 72 azure swords in his halo trembled, then flew out as countless azure threads that instantly reached the beetles.

"Clang, clang, clang!"

A series of clangs rang out as those azure threads struck, hurling the beetles through the air, but the beetles were still completely unharmed, like constructs of indestructible metal.

Yet this sudden pressure only enraged them more; they emitted piercing cries, spread wings, and shot away at incredible speed.

The azure threads were as fast as lightning but could not keep up, and thus could not hit the beetles.

The Han Li who had sent the azure threads seemed displeased. He formed a hand seal and flicked a finger at the giant azure cauldron.

A dull clang sounded, countless runes appeared over its surface, and simultaneously a tremendous invisible force surged from inside.

The fleeing beetles abruptly slowed; the intense golden light around their bodies dimmed completely.

By contrast the azure threads were also unhindered by this force; they accelerated and formed an azure light barrier over the entire cauldron.

Thus all thirteen became sitting targets and were lashed at by hundreds of azure threads.

Boings of metal on metal became heavy and dense like a torrential downpour, and dazzling blue light burst from their bodies. Their piercing screeches became even louder.

The expressions of all three Han Lis remained unchanged. Two of them rapidly made hand seals, pouring magic power into the cauldron in frenzy, while the last controlled the azure sword threads.

So the entire secret chamber became filled with the whirring of flying sword threads, mixed with booms and crackling explosions.

After an indeterminate stretch of time, the Han Li who had released the sword threads suddenly moved his sleeve; the azure threads stopped dead in the sky and instantly transformed back into the 72 azure swords in one flash.

The halo rotating above his head came to stillness and all flying swords vanished at once.

The halo itself vanished amid a dull thump.

At exactly the same moment the other two ceased forming hand seals, and the golden beetles, no longer suffocated by azure light, reappeared normally.

Even after being battered by sword threads for so long they were still untouched. Their rage had been mounting throughout; as soon as the cauldron restriction was lifted, they all flew ferociously toward the three Han Lis.

But the three Han Lis did not flinch. In perfect synchronicity they all cried one word.

"Stop!"

The golden beetles instantly halted mid-air; a burst of white light then shot from the azure cauldron, sweeping them up and carrying them back to it instantly.

The beetles were naturally unwilling to return, yet they had no strength to resist.

The one who had released the flying swords released a faint sigh and relaxed his expression, murmuring to himself,

"They are getting harder to control. The secret technique wasn't a lie; once they grow this much, the original control method no longer works. I'll need to hold another host-recognition ceremony. But at this stage, since all these beetles have similar power levels, feeding them repeatedly to make one devour another becomes highly unlikely." 

A slight frown appeared on Han Li's brow as he withdrew into thought.

Since leaving the infernal river, he had collected all rare materials he encountered for over a hundred years and had finally returned to the human world. Soon he understood the imminent devilish tribulation, so he gathered all three disciples and issued instructions before entering seclusion again.

His seclusion had two goals. First, to use Divine Infernal River Elixir (မြင့်မြတ်သော ငရဲမြစ် ဆေးလုံး) to improve his physical constitution and reach the peak of mid-Body Integration Stage before attempting breakthrough.

Second, to try nurturing a beetle monarch using the secret technique he had obtained from Voidspirit.

If he could succeed before the tribulation, he still might not be able to enter Elder Devil Realm without hesitation, but at least he could ensure his survival.

Though he suspected something odd about how easily Voidspirit gave that technique, he still chose to try it.

After being in the human world over one hundred years, he repeatedly studied the technique; combining his own insect-nurturing experiences and some unique advantages, he devised his own method.

His method was mostly similar to the Mayfly Race technique, with some small differences. For example, the Mayfly method required very rare spirit medicines fed only once due to rarity. But because Han Li had a mysterious small vial on him, he could mass-produce those medicines and feed the Gold Devouring Beetles regularly.

Also, some lightning types in the Mayfly technique were specified for refining their bodies; he substituted them with his own Divine Devilbane Lightning (မြင့်မြတ်သော နတ်ဆိုး ပျက်စီးသော မီးလိုင်း).

Naturally only the heavens would test whether these changes worked. Yet in Han Li's eyes, these changes should greatly increase the chance of success compared with the Mayfly method.

Over the years he kept around ten thousand unmutated mature Gold Devouring Beetles in reserve, while all the mutated ones were kept together. He had used special methods to repeatedly force them to devour one another.

To control these increasingly powerful beetles, he purchased this massive azure cauldron at a huge cost.

The cauldron was not very useful in ordinary battle, but it greatly strengthened his ability to control his Gold Devouring Beetles.

After all required preparations and near two hundred years of painstaking nurturing, the tens of thousands had been reduced to these final 13 Purple-striped Gold Devouring Beetles (ခရမ်းရောင် အစင်းပါ ရွှေရောင် စားသုံး ပိုးကောင်).

These new beetles all had potential to become Beetle Monarchs, and with combined power they could oppose even a normal mid-Body Integration cultivator.

Yet when Han Li tried to spur these 13 to continue consuming one another, he encountered an insurmountable obstacle. Because their power was so evenly matched, not one could kill another.
